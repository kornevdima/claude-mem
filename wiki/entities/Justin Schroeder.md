# Justin Schroeder

Engineer at [[StandardAgents]] (stealth, mid-2026); X handle @jpschroeder. Known for open-source projects: **Dmux** (terminal multiplexer for coding agents), **ArrowJS** (UI framework pitched as "React for the agentic era"), and (pre-agent era) FormKit for Vue.

## Position

Chief public advocate of [[Domain-Specific Agents]] — composition of small, complete, domain-scoped agents under a coordinator, instead of inflating one general-purpose agent's context with skills/MCP. Public prediction (AI Engineer talk, 2026-06-29): H2 2026 brings a rapid uptick in domain-specific-agent frameworks; **2027 is the year of multi-agent orchestration**. (Source: [[yt-schroeder-domain-specific-agents]])

Defines an agent as "deterministic software that harness the non-deterministic results produced by models in pursuit of some desired objective" and treats agent vs. harness as a pedantic distinction.
