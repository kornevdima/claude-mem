# Alex L. Zhang

Researcher at MIT CSAIL; lead author of **Recursive Language Models** (RLMs). Introduced RLMs in an October 2025 blog post, followed by the December 2025 paper (arXiv 2512.24601) with co-authors **Tim Kraska** and **Omar Khattab** (Khattab is also the author of DSPy, which now ships a `DSPy.RLM`).

## Significance

RLMs reframe long-context handling from "expand the window" to "store context in an environment and let the model program over it." This is the work [[Research Recursive Language Models]] evaluates for adoption in claude-mem's `wiki-query`.
